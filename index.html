FAQ

Straight answers—before you book.

Many patients self-refer for private MSK care. If you already have scans or letters, bring them— they can speed up understanding, but they are not always required to begin.

Ultrasound helps visualise the target tissue in real time. The clinic’s own messaging reflects research that guided injections can be more accurate than blind placement—supporting comfort and the likelihood of a successful result.

Hyaluronic acid, cortisone injections and platelet-rich plasma (PRP) are highlighted services on the current site. Suitability always depends on assessment, your medical history, and shared decision-making.

The clinic is based in Burnley; the existing website also speaks to Manchester-area patients seeking private MSK and injection care. Many people travel for a specialist-led appointment when waiting lists or uncertainty are the alternative.
